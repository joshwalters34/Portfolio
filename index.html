About
Me

My name is Josh Walters and I'm currently enrolled in a Full Stack Coding Bootcamp through UNC-Charlotte. I am a graduate UNC-Charlotte with a Communications degree. I've been working in the healthcare IT field for over 18 years. I worked at the UNC-Charlotte Student Health Center for 9 years. I was a Student Services Specialist and one of my duties was to test the EMR system that we utilized. I left the Student Health Center to pursue an opportunity with McKesson working on their EMR system, called Paragon. I worked as a Quality Assurance Analyst, testing the software before it was sent out to customers. During this time, I began to think about a career on the other side, actually doing the coding. I feel that my experience testing the software will give me a unique understanding of how to code the software.
